About Tomorrowland 2023

In July 2023, Tomorrowland celebrated its legendary 17th edition with 400.000 People of Tomorrow from over 200 nations gathering and uniting during two weekends of magic in the beautiful scenery of De Schorre in Boom, Belgium.

Theme: Adscendo

The 17th edition of Tomorrowland revolved around the theme 'Adscendo', turning De Schorre into a magical destination high on the horizon, where visitors witnessed the rise of a magnificent tale in the history of Tomorrowland's Great Library.
